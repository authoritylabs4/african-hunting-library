Welcome

The African Hunting Library is an independent educational resource created to help hunters better understand African hunting safaris. Rather than promoting specific operators or destinations, the goal is to explain how safaris work, how hunting is managed across Africa, how pricing is structured and what first-time hunters should know before planning a safari.

As new guides are published they will be added to this library to create a comprehensive collection of practical information for hunters researching African hunting opportunities.
